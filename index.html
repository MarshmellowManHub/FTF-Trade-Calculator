How FTF Hub Works

Stability

Stability indicates if an item's value is changing. Look for the icon in the top-left of items.

  • Rising: Value is going up.
  • Dropping: Value is going down.
  • Fluctuating: Unstable/Risky.
  • Stable: Consistent price.

Demand

Located in the top-right corner of items (e.g., D: 8).

  • 0 - 3: Low Demand (Hard to trade).
  • 4 - 6: Average Demand.
  • 7 - 10: High Demand (Easy to trade).

Value Types

We support two value systems. Toggle them in the settings or footer.

  • FV (Flee Value): The standard trading currency.
  • HV (Hammer Value): Raw value (approx. 40 FV = 1 HV).

Posting Ads

Logged-in users can post up to 4 trade ads per day.

  • Offer: Select up to 4 items you have.
  • Request: Select specific items you want, or choose "Upgrade", "Downgrade", or "Any".
  • Expiry: Ads auto-delete after 24 hours.
